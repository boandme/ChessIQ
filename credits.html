Welcome to ChessIQ!

Train your positional knowledge in chess and master the art of self-analysis. This skill is invaluable for chess players of all levels!


How To Play

You will be given a position. Analyze it and determine which side has the advantage:

  • White Winning: Click the White King
  • Black Winning: Click the Black King
  • Equal Position: Click the Equal symbol
